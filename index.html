Kelly Wilson-Stewart

About

I am a cath lab radiographer with advanced certification in both vascular and cardiac angiography.
I have worked in both public and private settings and have training in CT and MRI.
In addition to my clinical roles, I have spent 15 years as a university lecturer teaching undergraduate Medical Imaging.
I hold a Masters of Higher Education , and have submitted my final thesis which investigates
occupational dose to staff in fluoroscopically guided cardio-vascular procedures.
